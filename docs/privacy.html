SplitChaos Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

SplitChaos is a social bill-splitting app that uses Firebase for authentication, database sync, push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers from Firebase Authentication, such as anonymous user IDs or Apple Sign In identifiers.
  • Profile information you choose to add, such as your display name.
  • Group data, including group names, invite codes, members, expenses, settlement history, and related metadata.
  • Device and app data used for analytics, diagnostics, and push notifications, such as Firebase Analytics events, crash logs, and FCM tokens.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync groups, expenses, settlements, roles, and invites across devices in real time.
  • To send notifications about group activity, reminders, and app updates.
  • To improve the app, fix bugs, and monitor crashes and performance.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your group data is shared only with the people inside the groups you join or create. Firebase and Apple may process data on our behalf according to their own service terms and policies.

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account or group exists, or as needed to provide the service and meet legal or security requirements. You can delete a group from the app, and account-level deletion requests can be made by contacting us.

Children

SplitChaos is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13.
